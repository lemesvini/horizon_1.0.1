| Verb to Have and Verbs Eat and Drink

Replace "to Eat" or "to Drink" with "to Have"


I want to eat sushi I want to have sushi

I eat rice and beans I have rice and beans

You eat pasta for lunch You have pasta for lunch

We drink red wine We have red wine

Let's have ice cream!

| Third Person Rule

Add "S"

He works as a teacher.

She likes to eat sushi.

He loves to drink beer.

She plays cards with her grandma.

Add "Es"

Does he work as a teacher?

She watches X-men 97.

He washes the dishes.

She does not like ice cream.

Add "Ies"

For verbs ending in [Consonant + Y]

He studies economics.

She parties every weekend.

He terrifies her brother.
